Benefits of Android Root

Users who own an Android device can't stop from tweaking, changing, customizing, optimizing the internal system and OS. They want to get the most of their handsets by tweaking. And many advanced tweaks and tricks need root access to complete the processes. That's why so many user love to root their Android to get access to more functionality. As a newbie, you may afraid of rooting, beacuse rooting will immediately void your warranty of Android device. But these tweaks may be more appealing than the fear of lossing warranty. Once you have rooted your Android, you may never want to unroot it.
